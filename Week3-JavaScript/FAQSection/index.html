Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your visit.

What are your opening hours? +

We're open every day from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM, including weekends and holidays.

Do you offer delivery? +

Yes, we offer delivery within a 5km radius through our partner apps and direct orders.

Can I book a table in advance? +

Absolutely! You can call us or message us on social media to reserve a table.

Do you have vegan options? +

Yes, we have a range of vegan-friendly pastries and plant-based milk options for our drinks.
